<objective>
Add failure visibility for Cat and close the deal-subfolder gap in the CRM retry flow.

Purpose: When the Finmo webhook fires before MBP creates the opportunity, the system retries 3 times over 35 minutes. Currently, if all retries exhaust, it's log-only -- Cat has no visibility. This plan upgrades exhaustion to create a CRM task for Cat and also creates deal subfolders retroactively when a retry succeeds with a null dealSubfolderId.

Output: `createFailureTask` helper reusable across pipeline, wired into `processCrmRetry` exhaustion path, with deal subfolder catch-up on retry success.
</objective>
