## Current Gap

`finmoDocumentHandler()` is fully implemented in `src/intake/finmo-docs.ts` and exported from `src/intake/index.ts`, but no route in `server.ts` calls it. When clients upload docs directly to Finmo, the system doesn't know about it — Cat has to download from Finmo and forward to docs@ manually.

## Fix

One line in server.ts:
```typescript
app.post('/webhooks/finmo/documents', finmoDocumentHandler(intakeQueue));
```
